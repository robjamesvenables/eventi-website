✦ Features
Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don't.
Built by a hospitality professional, for hospitality businesses. Every feature solves a real operational problem — so your team spends less time managing and more time delivering.
📋
Live Event Plans / BEOs
Professional Banquet Event Orders that update in real time. Share via one link — no login required for clients. Replaces Google Docs permanently. Fully compatible with traditional Event Plan / BEO workflows.
🎤
Artist & Rider Management
Full Technical and Hospitality riders per artist. Stage plot uploads, DJ spec, dressing room, guest list — all in one card per artist.
🔧
Vendor Rental Workflow
Track every rental from quote to payment. Upload quotes, get approval, upload invoices, confirm payment. Full paper trail — digitised.
📅
Event Calendar
Month, list and day views. All events auto-populate from your BEOs. See your entire schedule at a glance.
📝
Meeting Notes
Shared team notepad — one entry per day, visible to everyone. Autosaves as you type. Copy to WhatsApp in one tap.
💬
WhatsApp Ready
Share your Event Plan / BEO, rundown or meeting notes directly to WhatsApp in one tap. Formatted, clean, professional. No screenshots needed.
💰
Finance & P&L
Auto-calculating finance table with IDR support, tax, vendor costs and net profit view. Know your event's worth before the doors open.
👥
Client Database
Import your client list via CSV or Excel. Every client linked to their event history. A lightweight CRM built for event teams.
🌍
133 Languages
Auto-detects your language on login. AI-powered translation covers every language worldwide. Switch instantly at any time.
